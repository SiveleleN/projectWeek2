ABOUT OUR FARMING INITIATIVE

The Problem

Pig farmers invest their resources to produce slaughter pigs, weaner piglets, and breeding pigs on an ongoing basis. They then struggle to find a market for their pigs.

The Solution

We help pig farmers sell their pigs online from the comfort of their farms. This website has been attracting South African pig buyers since it was created in 2020 and continues to be a reliable source for pig farmers to sell their pigs.
